My Outdoor Adventures

I love spending time outdoors, especially when it comes to fishing. There's something peaceful about being on the water and waiting for a bite.

Favorite Fishing Spots

  • Moyie River, Idaho
  • Bull Lake, Montana
  • Kootenai River, Idaho

Fishing Tips

Here are a few tips I've learned over the years:

  1. Be patient - sometimes it takes a while to get a bite.
  2. Use the right bait for the type of fish you're trying to catch. I enjoy fly fishing on the surface personally.
  3. Pay attention to the weather - fish are more active during certain conditions.
